Facts

A list of facts you may or may not know about Futurama.

The numbers on Bender's Door (00100100) translate to $ in binary.

Futurama was cancelled two times.
It was originally cancelled during the
fifth season and then was cancelled for
the last time during Season 7.

                    

 The ending of Jurrasic Bark doesn't happen after all 
due to Fry going back to the year 2000 in "Bender's

was due to Bender's failed attempt at killing Fry.
